What is Vishing Infrastructure as a Service?

We provide legitimate vishing (Voice phishing) infrastructure for red teaming & social engineering exercises. We save you from the hassle of setting up your own vishing infrastructure for these exercises where reliability is paramount.

What do we need?

Pre-requisites for conducting Vishing simulation

Approval from exercise conductors and those who are getting vished.

Time and duration of exercise, estimated sessions, target country and list of targets.

OpenVPN and Zoiper (Wireguard support coming soon).

How is it different from other providers?

We provide full spectrum vishing infrastructure which is managed by highly skilled operators with extensive experience in conducting red teaming exercises. Our low latency network is able to conduct vishing reliably across all geographies.

How fast can you give access to the infrastructure?

Infrastructure is ready within 30 minutes or less for conducting the exercise, post verification of your request.

How much does it cost?

Our Pricing starts at 150 Euros per exercise session of 1 hour. This includes a dedicated operator who will help you in setting up your infrastructure and will be there during the course of exercise. You will have access to a simple dashboard from where you can conduct vishing using the app of your choice.
